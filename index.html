The Memory Shop

An opshop selling donated clothing, shoes, books, and homewares.

All proceeds go to Alzheimers Auckland, helping to expand support services to meet the growing need that New Zealand faces with an ever-expanding elderly population.

Come pick up a bargain and help more people with dementia to live well.
